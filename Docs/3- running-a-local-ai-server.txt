1. Running a Remote Local AI Server:
    * On the Jetson, open 'Settings'
    * Go to the 'Network' tab and click the settings icon
    * Copy the 'IPv4 Address'
    * On another computer connected to the same local network:
        . Open a browser
        . Enter the Jetson's IP address followed by the port number
        . Log in using your credentials

    * The Jetson is now acting as a remote local AI server
